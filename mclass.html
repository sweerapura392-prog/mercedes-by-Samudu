Generations

W163 M-Class (1997–2005)

One of the first luxury SUVs to blend on-road comfort and off-road ability; body-on-frame with advanced AWD systems.

W164 M-Class (2005–2011)

Unibody platform, better dynamics and safety, improved interiors; AMG ML63 made a performance statement.

W166 M-Class (2011–2015)

Efficiency gains, ride improvements, driver assistance growth. In 2015, the line was renamed GLE.

W166/C292 GLE (2015–2019)

GLE SUV and GLE Coupé variants; expanded powertrains, advanced infotainment and safety suites.

W167 C167 GLE (2019–present)

MBUX, E-Active Body Control, hybridized powertrains, and AMG 53/63 models with formidable performance.
