Generations

W168 (1997–2004)

“Sandwich” floor with under-floor engine layout for crash safety and interior space. City-friendly packaging.

W169 (2004–2012)

Refinement boost, improved stability, and safety systems; continued compact MPV-style practicality.

W176 (2012–2018)

Radical shift to sporty hatch; sharper chassis and AMG A45 hot-hatch phenomenon.

W177/V177 (2018–present)

MBUX interface debut for the brand, upscale materials, driver assistance expansion; hatch and sedan variants.
