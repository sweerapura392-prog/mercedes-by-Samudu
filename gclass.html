Key Eras

W460/W461 (1979–present, utility)

Original Geländewagen: ladder frame, three locking diffs (later), and rugged simplicity for military and commercial use.

W463 (1990–2018)

Luxury interior, ABS/ESP integration, stronger engines including AMG V8s and V12s, yet kept the classic boxy design.

W463 (2018–present, new generation)

Completely reengineered chassis, independent front suspension, improved NVH, tech-rich cockpit, AMG G63 as halo.
